Radu's Story

Meet Radu, the charming and ever-so-talkative Orange Tabby! Radu is the beloved feline companion of his owner, Kal. With his striking orange fur and friendly demeanor, Radu is a well-known and adored member of the community in Port Credit.

Radu shares his home with his brother and sister, Ricky and Nelli. Radu, being the social butterfly he is, loves wandering around by the lake, greeting everyone he meets with his cheerful meows.

In February 2024, Radu faced a harrowing ordeal when he was stolen. It was a distressing time for Kal and everyone who loves Radu. However, thanks to the incredible efforts of Peel Regional Police, Radu was safely returned home in July 2024. The reunion was nothing short of miraculous, and Radu’s story touched the hearts of many.

Radu is not just a cat; he is a symbol of resilience and community spirit. His adventures, both joyful and challenging, have made him even more cherished by those who know him. Whether he’s lounging at home with Ricky and Nelli or exploring the great outdoors, Radu’s vibrant personality shines through. He continues to bring happiness to all who cross his path, and his heartwarming story serves as a reminder of the power of love and perseverance.
